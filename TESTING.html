T.R.U. Owl 3.0 Scoring

Scores measure civic credibility, not popularity.

The Scale

+1 to +10 = informs the public with observable facts.

0 = neutral or unverified record.

-1 to -10 = misrepresents facts, pushes fear, or subverts constitutional reality.

What Gets Scored

Truth: Facts and certified records vs. reality subversion.

Reliability: Audited records vs. direct manipulation.

Understanding: Clear context vs. fear-mongering.

Election Baseline

Major U.S. elections are decentralized, secure, and backed by paper trails. Baseless mass-fraud claims are scored as credibility damage.
