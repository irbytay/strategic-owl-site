Privacy Policy

Welcome to The Strategic Owl — where clarity matters, truth comes first, and your data stays yours.

🧠 What We Collect

Nothing personal. No tricks. No surveillance.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or location
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Login credentials — because we don’t even ask you to log in

We don’t care who you are — we care what you think and how we can help you think more clearly.

📡 How the App Works

The Strategic Owl pulls educational content from structured, read-only databases (like Google Sheets). These include facts, claims, and civic data — not user-submitted content.

We use this system to present you with:

  • Credibility scores
  • Misconception challenges
  • Constitutional reference tools
  • Truth-aligned insights

❌ No Ads. No Trackers. No Games.

We don’t use:

  • Biometric data

You’re not a product here. You’re a participant in reclaiming reality.
