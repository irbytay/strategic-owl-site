Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2025

Welcome to The Strategic Owl — where clarity matters, truth comes first, and your data stays yours.

This isn’t one of those apps that tracks you, profiles you, or sells you out. We don’t want your personal information. We don’t need it. We’re here to help you challenge misinformation — not become part of the data economy.

What We Collect

Nothing personal. No tricks. No surveillance.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or location
  • Your contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Login credentials — because we don’t even ask you to log in
  • Analytics tied to your identity

We don’t care who you are — we care what you think and how we can help you think more clearly.

How the App Works

The Strategic Owl pulls educational content from structured, read-only databases (like Google Sheets). These include facts, claims, and civic data — not user-submitted content.

We use this system to present you with:

  • Credibility scores
  • Misconception challenges
  • Constitutional reference tools
  • Truth-aligned insights

No Ads. No Trackers. No Games.

We don’t use:

  • Ad networks
  • Behavioral trackers
  • Social integrations
  • Location services
  • Biometric data

You’re not a product here. You’re a participant in reclaiming reality.

Safe for All Minds

This app is safe for all users, including those under 13. Since we don’t collect or store any user data, there’s nothing here for anyone to exploit.

Future Features

If we ever add optional features that involve third-party services or require permissions, you’ll know about it up front, and this policy will be updated accordingly.
