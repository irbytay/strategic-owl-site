The Strategic Owl

Welcome to the T.R.U. OWL Score.

This is a credibility scoring system — not based on partisanship, popularity, or punditry, but on verifiable truth.

In an age where narratives move faster than facts, we built this platform to see through the fog. Just like owls at night, our goal is clarity in darkness. Here, public figures are evaluated not by what they claim, but by what can be proven — through court records, public statements, correction history, and source-backed analysis.

We measure Truth, Reliability, and Understanding — and we hold different roles to different standards. Politicians aren’t journalists. Influencers aren’t elected leaders. Each is scored accordingly.

This project exists because truth matters more than ever. Not for clicks. Not for tribes. But for informed citizens in a culture at war with reality.

Use these scores. Share them. Challenge them. This is protected speech — and you’re now part of the conversation.

How Truth Is Verified

Our scores are grounded in subjects that are verifiably true. That means we don’t rely on vibes, viral opinions, or partisan claims—we rely on provable facts.

  • Historical events and public records
  • Official court rulings and government filings
  • Scientific consensus and peer-reviewed data
  • Directly documented statements, votes, and legislation

When a politician denies a court ruling, when an influencer misrepresents a constitutional amendment, or when a media outlet ignores its own retraction history—those are verifiable failures of truth, reliability, or understanding.

We don't penalize disagreement. We penalize distortion.
