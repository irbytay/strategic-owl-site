Voter Fraud Cases in the U.S.

According to the Heritage Foundation — a conservative group focused on election integrity — only 1,584 cases of voter fraud have been documented since 1982.

That total spans every state, every election, and every form of fraud over more than four decades.

This is their full public record — and even from a group aiming to show fraud is widespread, the data reveals just how rare it truly is.
